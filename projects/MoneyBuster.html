MoneyBuster


Designed to keep track of students' finance

The project was a part of the two-day Summer of Tech's Create Camp in Auckland 2019. I worked alongside with developers and mentors in order to finish a WebApp in less than two days. The theme of the design is to be simple to use and minimalistic to allow the interface to be intuitive and easy to use for everyday lives. The application could be extends by adding in function to allow users to scan their receipts or set a saving goal.
