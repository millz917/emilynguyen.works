Put Poverty in the Past

This is an ongoing voluntary project for P3 Foundation Auckland. My role as a designer is to raise awareness of poverty issues in New Zealand and in the world through my design. The design is also not limited to digital or print. I was able to explore a variety of medium from social media campaign to using CMS (NationBuilder) to creating posters and annual report document.
